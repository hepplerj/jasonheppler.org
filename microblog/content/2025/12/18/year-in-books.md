Here's my reading log for 2025. As usual, my reading log doesn't capture the full extent of everything I've read over the year since I don't necessarily read cover to cover or log everything I consult. A bit lighter on the fiction versus [last year](https://writing.jasonheppler.org/2024/12/28/year-in-books-for/), but I greatly enjoyed Robin Sloan's works and re-reading the *Lord of the Rings* trilogy. I think my favorite reads this year were Dan Flores' *Coyote America* and *Wild New World*--stellar and engaging environmental histories through deep time.
