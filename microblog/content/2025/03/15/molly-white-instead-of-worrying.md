[Molly White](https://www.citationneeded.news/free-and-open-access-in-the-age-of-generative-ai/): 

> Instead of worrying about "wait, not like that", I think we need to reframe the conversation to "wait, not _only_ like that" or "wait, not in ways that threaten open access itself". The true threat from AI models training on open access material is not that more people may access knowledge thanks to new modalities. . . . It's that trillion dollar companies become the sole arbiters of access to knowledge after subsuming the painstaking work of those who made knowledge free to all, killing those projects in the process.
