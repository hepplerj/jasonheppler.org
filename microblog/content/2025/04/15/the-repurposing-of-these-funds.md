> The repurposing of these funds represents not only a change in priorities but a fundamental shift of values for the federal government. Where the NEH has supported inquiry, the National Garden of American Heroes demands reverence. . . . When a culture moves from inquiry to reverence—with tens of millions of dollars facilitating the shift—it becomes propagandistic. Instead of fueling the passionate work of researchers and writers and curators, the same pot of money will be turned, literally, into stone.
