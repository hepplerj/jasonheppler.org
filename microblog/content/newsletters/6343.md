<p>📨 Mail Client: Apple Mail<br>

📆 Calendar: Apple &amp; Google Calendar<br>
📁 Cloud File Storage: iCloud, Dropbox, Backblaze<br>

📜 Word Processing: Obsidian, Pages, Word if I have to<br>
📈 Spreadsheets: Numbers, Excel if I have to<br>
📊 Presentations: Keynote<br>
🛒 Shopping Lists: Apple Reminders<br>
