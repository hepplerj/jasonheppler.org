<p>A lusher, less-barren canyon might not sound like a bad thing. Sand shields these sites, which include stone structures, slab-lined granaries and craterlike roasting pits, from weather and the elements. With less sand drifting up from the riverside, the sites are more exposed to erosion and trampling by visitors.</p> </blockquote>
