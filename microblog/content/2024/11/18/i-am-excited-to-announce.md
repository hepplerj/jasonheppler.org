I am excited to announce the launch of [The Denig Manuscript](https://denigmanuscript.org), a collaboration between RRCHNM and Winterthur. The manuscript is an illuminated Bible created by the Pennsylvania merchant Ludwig Denig, and the project includes translations, full size images, forensic analysis, and scholarship.
