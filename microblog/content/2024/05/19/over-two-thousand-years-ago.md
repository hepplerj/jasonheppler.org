Over two thousand years ago Herodotus, who we often consider the father of our profession, wrote an unusually detailed depiction of an unusual boat. Historians assumed it wasn't real. [Then archeologists found wreckage of such a boat](https://www.theguardian.com/science/2019/mar/17/nile-shipwreck-herodotus-archaeologists-thonis-heraclion).
