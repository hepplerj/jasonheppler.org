Saddened to learn that my MA advisor and mentor passed last night. His graduate courses were among my favorites and most influential in making me the historian I am today. We organized a "roast" of him at a conference a handful of years ago, which was a wonderful celebration of his work and influence. Having moved back to Lincoln, I was just thinking about him the other day and intended to look him up to see if we could grab lunch. He was a great force in the world.
