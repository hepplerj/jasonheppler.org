"I cannot overemphasize how much the lesson of the web is that people, given the choice between the freedom of operating and managing their own platform, and running a centralized platform that they do not control, [will choose the centralized platform](https://ftrain.medium.com/web-conversation-from-the-other-side-5f3881bfaad8). The desire of regular people with things to do to also become systems administrators is far less than what we assumed it would be."
