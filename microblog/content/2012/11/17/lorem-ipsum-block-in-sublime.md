Awesome little feature in Sublime Text:

{{< highlight console >}}
lorem + Tab
{{< / highlight >}}

Instant Lorem Ipsum paragraph. I use a custom ipsum generator in TextExpander (because I'm nerdy like that), but little things like this make Sublime Text wonderful.
