Making gløgg today:

- 1 cinnamon stick
- 6 cloves
- 1 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
- 3 cardamom pods
- 3 whole peppercorns 
- 1 bottle red wine
- 1/3 cup aquavit
- 1 tbsp honey
- orange zest 
- 1 handful of raisins
- 1 handful chopped almonds

Combine everything except zest and honey. Heat through on a stove, but _don’t boil_, for fifteen minutes. Add remaining ingredients and serve.
