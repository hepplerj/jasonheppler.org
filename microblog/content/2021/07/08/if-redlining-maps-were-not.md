“If redlining maps were not just maps of race, then what were they maps of? They were maps that cataloged and reproduced the spatial order of a much broader system of inequality and injustice, one in which [race mingled with other markers of social disinvestment](https://www.dissentmagazine.org/online_articles/redlining-race-and-the-color-of-money).”
