Silicon Valley and the Environmental Inequalities of High-Tech Urbanism

How did the world's most celebrated landscape of innovation become one of its most polluted?

A century of growth, water, and waste beneath the Valley's orchards-turned-office-parks — and the communities who bore the environmental costs of high tech.

{{/* Governing Ground */}}

Digital History

Nearly a third of the American West is federal land — a century-long provocation. Governing Ground is a digital history of the Sagebrush Rebellions, the recurring movements from the 1930s to the present over the transfer, ownership, and control of federal lands.
