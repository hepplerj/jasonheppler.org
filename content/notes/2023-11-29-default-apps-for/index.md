There have been a bunch of "what apps am I using?" blog posts recently, inspired by [an episode of Hemispheric Views](https://listen.hemisphericviews.com/097). Robb Knight [has a page with links](https://defaults.rknight.me/) to other people’s posts.

Here’s my list:

📨 Mail Client: Apple Mail  
📮 Mail Server: iCloud, Fastmail  
📝 Notes: Obsidian  
✅ To-Do: Things 3, Apple Reminders, Github Issues  
📷 iPhone Photo Shooting: Apple Camera  
🌅 Photo Management: Apple Photos  
📆 Calendar: Apple & Google Calendar  
📁 Cloud File Storage: iCloud, Dropbox, Backblaze  
📖 RSS: NetNewsWire  
🙍🏻‍♂️ Contacts: Apple Contacts  
🌐 Browser: Safari, Firefox  
💬 Chat: Messages, Signal, Slack  
🔖 Bookmarks: In something of a shock to even me, the browser  
📑 Read It Later: Matter  
📜 Word Processing: Obsidian, Pages, Word if I have to  
📈 Spreadsheets: Numbers, Excel if I have to  
📊 Presentations: Keynote  
🛒 Shopping Lists: Apple Reminders  
🍴 Meal Planning: Nothing (shoutout to [Mela](https://mela.recipes))  
💰 Budgeting and Personal Finance: Monarch  
📰 News: Safari, NetNewsWire  
🎵 Music: Apple Music  
🎙️ Podcasts: Overcast  
🔐 Password Management: Apple Passwords, 1Password  
🤖 Code Editing: VSCode, nvim  
📚 Books: So many, almost always paper (very occasionally Apple Books)  
🌎 Blogging: Micro.blog, Hugo
